Petr Kouba

I'm a PhD student working jointly at CIIRC in Prague under the supervision of Josef Šivic and Jiří Sedlář and at Loschmidt Laboratories in Brno under the supervision of Stas Mazurenko. The focus of my research is Machine Learning for Proteins. In particular, I'm interested in machine learning methods for protein design and in methods utilizing data on protein dynamics. Before starting my PhD, I obtained an MSc degree in Computer Vision from CTU in Prague and an MSc degree in Physics from ETH Zurich.
